Crackpot team from TR headed up by tech lead and top thinker, Michelle finally put one the World's greatest mysteries to rest - well done M! finally arrived home.

In her own words, Michelle says "I was about to eat 10 pieces of pasta when my knife and fork fell from my hands and landed on the floor in an 'X' pattern! I was taken aback but didn't let it overwhelm me - I stayed calm and focussed in on what must be done! It became clear to me this was significant so I called for an emergency session of TR hi-tech council in the square room and quickly the team entered into hot debate as to the explanantion of this strange phenomenon and if it had really any signicance."

"Insticntively I knew that X represented 10 becasue X must equal 10 becasue there were 10 pieces of pasta" she continues. "We stayed up all night until at 4.50am when we made a second breakthrough - it was L-ate at 50 minutes past 4 in the morning so L was 50!

Rather modestly Michelle said finally "I could go on but won't blow my own trumpet any longer than necessary but needless to say I...er, we got there in the end, er...apart from ZERO - we haven't figured that one out yet!"
