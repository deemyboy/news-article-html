Roman Numerals - Finally TR Unlock the Age-Old Mystery!

Crackpot team from TR headed up by tech lead and top thinker, Michelle finally put one the World's greatest mysteries to rest - well done M! "Our gift to The World!"

In her own words, Michelle describes the moment that will alter the course of human history... "I was about to eat 10 pieces of pasta when my knife and fork fell and landed in an 'X' pattern! I was taken aback obviously but didn't let it overwhelm me as I immediately realised this represented a turning point in our quest to finally crack the Roman Numeral code!

Looking back I'm not sure how but I managed to stay calm and focussed-in on what needed to be done so I called for an emergency session of TR Hi-Tech Council in the Square Office and shortly the team had assembled and entered into hot debate as to the explanantion of this strange phenomenon and if it had any real meaning."

"Insticntively I knew that X represented 10 becasue there were 10 pieces of pasta and so I stuck to my guns, occasionally firing the odd bullet to quieten dissenters, until I had rammed the point home to the remaining members of the Council who realised it was better to concede than face the consequences!

She continues. "After that, the few who remained stayed up all night until at 4.50am we made a second breakthrough! I happened to glance at the time as it was getting L-ate at 50 minutes past 4 in the morning so L had to be 50... DIDN'T IT EVERYONE! Not too many arguments to be fair and so we continued in this fashion till we had finally solved the whole mystery!"

Rather modestly Michelle said finally "I could go on but won't blow my own trumpet any longer than necessary but needless to say I...er, we got there in the end, er ...apart from ZERO - we haven't figured that out yet!"
